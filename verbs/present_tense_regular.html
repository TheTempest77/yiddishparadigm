Simple Present Tense

In Yiddish, the simple present tense may be used to express a habitual action or a present continuous action.

Habitual

ער לױףט אַלע טאָג

He runs every day

Present Continuous

איך גײ אין דער קראָם

He is walking to the store

Paradigm

Every regular verb will have a base form from which every conjugation will be formed. It is worth noting that verbs are generally classified by their infinitives rather than their base forms, so the infinitive form will be included in this paradigm.

From the base form, the following conjugations are formed:

Infinitive ן-
Singular Plural

2p סט- ט-
3p ט- ן-
